Common questions

Answers before you ask.

Will this replace my front desk staff?
No — and that's by design. The AI handles overflow and after-hours calls that your team physically can't get to. Your staff stays exactly as they are, focused on patients in the chair. This is a support layer, not a replacement.
We already have a booking system. Does this work with it?
Yes. The agent integrates with Google Calendar, Calendly, Dentrix, Eaglesoft, and Open Dental. Setup takes 48 hours and I handle everything — your team doesn't touch anything technical.
What happens if the AI can't handle a call?
Anything urgent, sensitive, or outside the agent's scope gets escalated immediately to your team. The agent is designed to know its limits — patients never get stuck in a loop.
Will patients know they're talking to an AI?
Most don't notice — and they don't need to. The agent answers with your clinic's name, uses a natural voice, and handles the conversation professionally. What patients care about is getting their appointment booked quickly, and that's exactly what happens.
Is this HIPAA compliant?
Yes. The agent handles intake only — no diagnosis, no treatment advice. Call data is handled securely and the system is built with patient privacy as a non-negotiable.
How long does setup take?
48 hours from sign-on to live. I handle all the configuration, branding, and calendar integration. You don't need to do anything technical — just forward your overflow calls and the agent takes it from there.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel any time, no questions asked. There's no long-term contract — just a flat monthly fee. If it's not working for your clinic, you walk away. Simple as that.
Why $297/month? Is that negotiable?
$297 is a flat rate that covers everything — setup, support, updates, and unlimited calls. To put it in perspective: one extra patient captured per month generates $7,000+ in lifetime value. The math works heavily in your favor.
